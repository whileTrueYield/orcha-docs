## Notes

A personal scratch space for things that don't belong in a ticket. Meeting notes, quick thoughts, links to revisit later. Notes are private to you and always accessible from the sidebar. Not everything needs to be tracked, estimated, and scheduled ; sometimes you just need a place to write something down.

Assign a color to each note to keep things organized, six colors to choose from, and you can rename each color to whatever makes sense for your workflow (Personal, Meetings, Ideas, Follow-ups, whatever fits).

## Search

Global search across your entire workspace. Find tickets by title or content, locate projects by name, look up team members. Results are instant and scoped to what you have access to.

Search is the fastest way to navigate a workspace that's grown beyond what you can hold in your head. Instead of clicking through folder hierarchies or scrolling through boards, type what you're looking for and go directly there.
