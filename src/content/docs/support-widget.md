---
title: Support Widget
description: An embeddable widget that turns user feedback into Orcha tickets automatically.
---

Support requests that live in email threads and Slack DMs get lost. The support widget gives your users a structured way to reach you, and routes everything into Orcha where it gets tracked like any other work.

## What it does

The widget renders as an iframe you embed on your site or app. It provides three things:

- **Contact form** -- users describe their issue, and the submission creates a ticket in Orcha automatically
- **Documentation search** -- surfaces relevant help articles before they submit, which can deflect common questions
- **Screenshot upload** -- users can attach a screenshot for context, so you spend less time asking "what did you see?"

## How submissions land

Every submission creates a ticket in Orcha, tagged with the source widget. From there it follows your normal triage flow, prioritize it, assign it, and let the scheduler fit it into your timeline.
