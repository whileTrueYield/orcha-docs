title: Account Deactivation
description: When someone leaves the team, their work gets redistributed automatically -- no orphaned tickets, no manual cleanup.
---

People leave teams. When they do, their assigned tickets don't just disappear -- they need to land on someone else's plate. Most tools leave you with a pile of orphaned work and a morning of manual reassignment.

## How deactivation works

When you deactivate a team member, Orcha redistributes all their assigned tickets across the remaining team. Autopilot re-simulates with the new team composition, and you see updated ETAs immediately.

The deactivated member's history -- comments, time logs, past assignments -- stays intact. Nothing is deleted. They just stop being an active participant in scheduling.

## No orphaned work

Every ticket that was assigned to the departing member gets a new owner. The schedule adjusts for the redistribution, including any downstream dependencies. You see the full impact in one pass, not after discovering unassigned tickets three weeks later.
